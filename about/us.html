About Us

AKNAFLOP is an online micro finance company that provides technologically driven, cost effective and accessible short term online loans ranging from R50 - R1000 and up to 2 months repayment period.Our service is rendered online though our web site aknaflop.com. Although AKNAFLOP was launched only Two years ago, the company has experienced greater than anticipated demand for its service. We run under competent entrepreneurial and visionary management that has ambitious future plans and believes in on going innovation in the micro finance industry. We have achieved a lot by paying attention to our customers.We inspire people to ask questions, learn more about their credit record, how to keep it updated and to know how much they are paying before applying for, not only with AKNAFLOP but with any other Micro lending company. In addition, we allow the customer to engage with us through our website to build a trust based relationship with us. Our customers trust us and know that we will suggest solutions that will help them to save time and money. We provide peace of mind, which our customers value the most. We realize that privacy is an important consideration for every customer. That is why we ensure that your personal information will be secure.



Should you have emergency expenses to cover, we can offer you a short-term loan that will give you financial breathing space. We offer quick and easy short-term loans to help you manage your cash flow and eliminate month end stresses.


Our innovative and responsive online platform means that you can apply for a loan at whatever time is most convenient to you. You are in control and you can choose the time that will be most beneficial to you. This means that you can invest enough time to accurately record your financial and personal situation and comprehensively consider which loan is best for your circumstances.


In order to qualify for a loan, you will need to be a registered student/employed and receive a regular allowance/salary. All loans are granted after your income and expenses have been verified. A credit check will be performed – however if you have bad credit, it doesn’t automatically disqualify you from being granted a loan. We take many factors into consideration before deciding on your suitability for a loan. Providing we are satisfied that you will be able to repay the borrowed amount, we will certainly consider your application.
